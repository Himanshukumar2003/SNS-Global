About

SNS Global

SNS Global is a premier MICE, events, hospitality, and travel solutions provider, delivering exceptional experiences across the world. A vertical of Sun & Sand Tours & Travels, a renowned 25+ year-old travel agency, we bring unmatched expertise in end-to-end travel management, including ticketing, hotel bookings, transportation, visas, and concierge services.

From corporate conferences and exhibitions to luxury destination weddings and high-profile social events, SNS Global combines strategic execution with seamless hospitality, ensuring every detail is meticulously planned and flawlessly delivered.

Our Legacy

With a strong foundation in the travel and hospitality industry, SNS Global benefits from the deep-rooted experience and global network of Sun & Sand Tours & Travels. Over the years, we have expanded our capabilities, offering a holistic approach to events and business travel that caters to corporate and luxury clients worldwide.

Our Mission

To redefine global events and travel experiences by integrating world-class event planning, hospitality expertise, and customized travel solutions.

Our Vision

To be the preferred partner for MICE, events, and hospitality solutions, delivering seamless, high-impact experiences that set new industry benchmarks.

​​Our Services

SNS Global offers a comprehensive suite of MICE, event management, hospitality, and travel solutions, ensuring seamless execution for corporate, luxury, and high-profile clients. Whether it's a global conference, an incentive trip, or an exclusive brand showcase, we handle every aspect with expertise and precision.
