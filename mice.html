MICE

MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions) & Event Management Services focus on planning and executing corporate events, business gatherings, and large-scale exhibitions. These services include venue selection, logistics, travel arrangements, and event coordination to ensure seamless experiences. Companies use MICE services to enhance brand visibility, foster business relationships, and create engaging experiences for attendees.

A professional event management team ensures that every aspect, from planning to execution, is handled efficiently. From high-tech event solutions to on-site management and customized experiences, MICE services help organizations create impactful and well-structured events that leave a lasting impression.

Incentive Travel & End-to-End Management

Reward and motivate your teams with exclusive, tailor-made incentive trips to stunning global destinations. We manage flights, hotels, experiences, team-building activities, and concierge services, making each journey unforgettable.

Buyer-Seller Meets

We specialize in organizing structured B2B buyer-seller meetings, facilitating networking opportunities, business matchmaking, and industry collaborations. From sourcing the right buyers to managing appointments and event logistics, we create productive engagements for maximum ROI.

Exhibition Space Booking & Selling

Leverage our expertise in booking and selling exhibition spaces at global trade shows, ensuring optimal visibility and engagement for your brand. We assist with booth selection, sponsorship opportunities, and strategic positioning to maximize footfall and lead generation.

SNS Global specializes in MICE, event management, hospitality, and travel solutions. We offer corporate conferences, luxury weddings, PR & communications, and seamless travel management. From venue selection to event execution, we ensure flawless experiences across India, UAE, and beyond.
