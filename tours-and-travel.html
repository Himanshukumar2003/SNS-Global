Tours & Travel

At Sun & Sand Tours & Travels, we specialize in providing comprehensive travel services across the globe, ensuring seamless and memorable experiences for every traveler. Whether you are planning a corporate tour, an exclusive event, or a personal getaway, we offer tailored solutions to meet your needs. Our expertise spans across flight bookings, hotel accommodations, visa assistance, customized itineraries, and luxury travel arrangements. With a strong network of global partners, we guarantee the best travel deals, premium services, and hassle-free journeys. From exotic beach destinations to bustling metropolises, we curate experiences that align with your preferences. Our dedicated team ensures that every trip is meticulously planned, allowing you to travel with confidence and comfort. Whether for business or leisure, let us take care of your travel needs while you create unforgettable memories!

Incentive Travel & End-to-End Management

Reward and motivate your teams with exclusive, tailor-made incentive trips to stunning global destinations. We manage flights, hotels, experiences, team-building activities, and concierge services, making each journey unforgettable.

Buyer-Seller Meets

We specialize in organizing structured B2B buyer-seller meetings, facilitating networking opportunities, business matchmaking, and industry collaborations. From sourcing the right buyers to managing appointments and event logistics, we create productive engagements for maximum ROI.

Exhibition Space Booking & Selling

Leverage our expertise in booking and selling exhibition spaces at global trade shows, ensuring optimal visibility and engagement for your brand. We assist with booth selection, sponsorship opportunities, and strategic positioning to maximize footfall and lead generation.

Travel Services Across the Globe

  • Luxury & Business Hotel Bookings
  • Visa Processing & Documentation
  • Ground Transportation & Chauffeur Services
  • Personalized Itineraries & Concierge Services
