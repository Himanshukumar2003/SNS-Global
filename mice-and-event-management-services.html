MICE & Event Management Services

MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions) & Event Management Services focus on planning and executing corporate events, business gatherings, and large-scale exhibitions. These services include venue selection, logistics, travel arrangements, and event coordination to ensure seamless experiences. Companies use MICE services to enhance brand visibility, foster business relationships, and create engaging experiences for attendees. A professional event management team ensures that every aspect, from planning to execution, is handled efficiently, delivering a successful and memorable event.

Venue Research & Booking

Selecting the perfect venue is key to an event’s success. Our team identifies, negotiates, and books top-tier venues worldwide, from luxury hotels to convention centers, ensuring they align with your event goals and brand image.

Event Planning & Production

From concept to execution, we handle all aspects of event planning, including theme development, logistics, stage production, entertainment, and technology integration. Whether it's a corporate summit, product launch, or gala dinner, we ensure an immersive and seamless experience.

Incentive Travel & End-to-End Management

Reward and motivate your teams with exclusive, tailor-made incentive trips to stunning global destinations. We manage flights, hotels, experiences, team-building activities, and concierge services, making each journey unforgettable.

Buyer-Seller Meets

We specialize in organizing structured B2B buyer-seller meetings, facilitating networking opportunities, business matchmaking, and industry collaborations. From sourcing the right buyers to managing appointments and event logistics, we create productive engagements for maximum ROI.

Exhibition Space Booking

Leverage our expertise in booking and selling exhibition spaces at global trade shows, ensuring optimal visibility and engagement for your brand. We assist with booth selection, sponsorship opportunities, and strategic positioning to maximize footfall and lead generation.

Travel Services Across the Globe

As a vertical of Sun & Sand Tours & Travels, we offer end-to-end travel solutions for corporate groups, events, and individual travelers, including:

  • International & Domestic Ticketing
  • Luxury & Business Hotel Bookings
  • Visa Processing & Documentation
  • Ground Transportation & Chauffeur Services
  • Personalized Itineraries & Concierge Services
